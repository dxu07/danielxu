Making Jinx's Minigun IRL

For this project I am attempting to make a working version of Jinx's minigun in real life. I am working on using Solidworks to design all the internal airsoft components from scratch and the minigun itself. Most of these parts will be manufactured using my 3D printers (Bambulab A1 and Tevo Tornado) with PLA. Below are some progress pics of the design.
